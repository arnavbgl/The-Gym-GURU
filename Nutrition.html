Iron

Iron is an essential mineral required for:

  • Production of hemoglobin
  • Transportation of oxygen in the body
  • Cellular energy production
  • Supporting cognitive function

Vitamin C

Vitamin C, also known as ascorbic acid, plays a crucial role in:

  • Collagen synthesis
  • Wound healing
  • Antioxidant function
  • Boosting the immune system

Calcium

Calcium is vital for:

  • Bone and teeth health
  • Muscle function
  • Nerve signaling
  • Blood clotting
